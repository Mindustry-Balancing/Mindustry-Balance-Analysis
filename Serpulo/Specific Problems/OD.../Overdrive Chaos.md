## Overdrive Chaos

### Overdrive Problem Set (Draft)

> For unit-turret,
> - (Strong) Overdrive favors turrets because, at the vitality stage, it is very clear that 2x units =/= unit with double health and dps.
> - (Strong) Because of this advantage, if you try to balance units around overdrive rates, you end up with overdrive feeling very forced for 2 reasons.
> 1. The first is that if you try to make t5... feel t5 (big, strong), you need both the unit dps and cost to feel that way, and any overdriving of eco is mandatory to make it cheap enough for regular standards.
> 2. The second is that if the turret defense doesn't have it, they just die.
> 
> For eco,
> - (Weak) Overdrive basically makes everything cheaper. If its 2.5x boost, it means 2.5x the items, and it means the value of each item is 1/2.5x. There is a limit on how high the boost can be to make items... feel meaningful
> - (Strong) The above leads to forcing yourself to use the best overdrive in the fastest time possible. Why ever stay and only use overdrive or pod, when you can rush overdrive dome and get payback insanely fast with insane item value drops?
> - (Strong) Overdrive seems too easy to setup, its like if erekir had only eheater and its really cheap. While it is fun to have enhanced production, don't you feel like its missing something meaningful to give time and effort for?

### Refined Resolving List;

1. Current return of Investment is insanely efficient. **Dome requires less than a tile of thorium ore to maintain.** Silicon, Power upkeeps and Upfront Cost are laughably low. Same for the projector, especially at base.
2. #1 drives meta direction towards obtaining the huge boost Dome/Phase projector provides. Their significance is objectively better than expanding economy via space use, especially in tight areas ( megabase sectors landing space, for ex)
3. Like #2, this also makes the Effectiveness of Defense way too wide in terms of potential ability independant of _base stats and upfront cost_. (we can't 2.5x units, since non-overdriven defenses would then be underpowered af)

An overdrive Nerf, coupled with the next few paragraphs, would fix those issues to a great extent.


### Potential overdrive nerf adjustments;

- Proportional T4+ Unit Production Upfront, Upkeep and Speed Buffs.
This will prevent army-building from being too time-consuming and tedious. Very dependent on Current OD.

- Post-Plastanium tech Turret Fixes (Plast Nerf, Surge Buff)
Obviously, spectre underperforms atrociously compared to the other two surge-tier turrets. Swarmercyclone heavily rely on their "expensive ammo" and synergies to justify absurd DPS values relative to surge tier.
One thorium is ~27.5 times better at Defense Uptime towards powering Meltdowns than loading Spectres. Yep

- Surge Smelter, Phase Weaver Upfront+Upkeep/Airblast Upfront Cost Nerfs.
In order to not press all the Upfront nerfs on Overdrive blocks alone, Endgame crafters should have their Build+Power Cost increased. This will hinder the ability to deploy boosts extremely early in competitive gameplay, while also stabilizing the much-too-easy jump of thorium->surge tech pacing.
